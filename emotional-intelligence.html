Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Definition: Emotional intelligence refers to become aware of or concicous, control and evalute of emotions. some studies says it is an inborn quality that person can born with but it can be learned and develop with slef regulation and motivation. like when we perceive emotions, we should use those emotions to promote thinking and cognative activity. it can only be achieved with understanding and managing our emotions.

How is it different to IQ?

Intelligence Quotient is the person ability to learn, understand and apply information and skills they have developed throughout the learning process and used all in meaningful way. whereas Emotional Intelligence is quality how a person recognizes emotions and understand and manage emotions. A person with high EQ means someone is self-confident, self-aware and able to handle difficult emotional experiences.

Why is it important?

Emotional Intelligence has big impacts in our life it help us to manage stress and emotions in life. when we have good control on our emotions, we can experss our feeling in better way and understand others feeling too. It helps us in making good decisions in our life. EQ has impact on our health too if we can't control our stress.
